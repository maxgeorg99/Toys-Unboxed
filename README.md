This Project is my submission for the Bad Ideas Game Game 2026 
https://itch.io/jam/bad-ideas-game-jam-2026 

A Short explaining the Game convept will follow.

The Theme of this Jam is "Outside the box". 

In this game you play as Matthew a little kid which found a old box of his fathers in the dark cellar.
After he opened the box in his kids room a magician apeared and a bunch of soldieres, skeletons 
and other mythical creature toy's fall out of the box.

The magician enchants the toy's and they become alive! 

You play ontop of the Box in a tacticle Autobattler.

With a Rock, Paper and Sicor system you have to adjust to the decisions of your enemy in a round based PVP game.

Inspired by Mechabelum and Demeon.
